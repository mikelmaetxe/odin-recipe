Spanih omelette

Description

Spanish omelette is the absolute king of spanish cuisine. It is a cheap and delicious dish.

Altough for every single spaniard the best omelette recipe is his grandma's, I will try to explain the most common way to prepare it

Ingredients

  • 10 eggs
  • 1 onion
  • 2 big potatoes

Steps

  1. First, peel the potatos and chop them in small axial slices
  2. Fry the potatos in a pan until they are really soft
  3. Chop the onion in small slices and fry them
  4. Mix the fried potatoes and onion in a bowl with the eggs
  5. Cook the mix in a pan for 10 minutes each side
